Jinsung Hitec Vina

JinSung Hitec Vina Co., Ltd
Ngày: 08/12/2022 11:06
Jinsung Hitec Vina, established in 2021 with over 300 employees, supplies materials for the supporting industry. During a project survey, experts noted the company’s initial energy-saving practices, such as using inverters, LED lighting, and promoting energy-conscious behavior. The company is also planning a 1,000m² plastic molding workshop with new machinery powered by electricity. The project management team proposed support activities, including assistance in preparing financing proposals for access to preferential loans and investment funds, technical training on practical energy efficiency solutions, and other tailored support under the project framework to align with the company’s needs and growth direction.

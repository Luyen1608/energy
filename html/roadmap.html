ESCO Roadmap

Roadmap to Establish ESCO Association in Vietnam

Energy Service Companies (ESCOs) play a crucial role in promoting energy efficiency by offering comprehensive energy-saving solutions, including energy audits, financing, implementation, and maintenance of energy-saving projects. In Vietnam, the ESCO model has been identified as a potential driver for scaling energy efficiency (EE) projects, particularly in sectors like manufacturing, food processing, and real estate. However, the ESCO business in Vietnam is still in its nascent stage due to several factors. In recent years, several pilot projects have been initiated in Vietnam to test the ESCO model, particularly in industries like manufacturing and commercial real estate. However, scaling these pilots to broader adoption remains a challenge without more structured support.
The establishment of an ESCO Association in Vietnam is essential for creating an enabling environment for the ESCO model to thrive. A clear roadmap for establishing the ESCO Association in Vietnam is vital for ensuring structured development, aligning stakeholders, and addressing policy and regulatory gaps that hinder the growth of the ESCO industry.

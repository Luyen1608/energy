Objectives

01 .
Enhance Energy Efficiency in supporting and food processing industries through increasing public and private-sector awareness of RE & EE practice
02 .
Supporting the private sector to access financing for their Energy Efficiency investments
03 .
Networking among manufacturers, financiers and Energy Service Companies (ESCOs)
04 .
Piloting an Energy Efficiency bench-marking for Supporting and Food Processing Industries in Vietnam
05 .
Developing a road map for the establishment of an ESCO Association in Vietnam.

Project Key focus areas

Capacity building for supporting and food processing manufacturers
EE network development
A scale-able EE bench-marking tool is piloted in factories 
A road map for an ESCO Association establishment
Policy recommendations

Expected outcomes

100 Food processing and supporting Manufacturers have improved management practices to more efficient energy consumption in production and a better ultilisation of available energy resources
10 Factories Supported in developing bankable energy efficiency projects
> 3 Industries enterprises Adopting improved energy efficiency technologies
